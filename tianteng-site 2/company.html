About Us

Shenzhen Tianteng Digital Technology Co., Ltd. was established in 2005 as an agent and distributor of storage products. Our business covers a comprehensive range of storage solutions — solid-state drives, portable SSDs, portable hard drives, flash memory cards and USB flash drives — sourced from the industry's most trusted brands including Samsung, Kingston, ADATA, Netac, Western Digital and Crucial.

With over seven years of verified trading history and more than 30,000 completed transactions, we combine fast, responsive service with reliable, on-time global shipping. "Great Service is Our Mission" is more than a slogan — it is the standard we hold ourselves to on every order, from single units to bulk and OEM procurement.
